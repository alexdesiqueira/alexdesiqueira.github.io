Hey! I’m Alex

I am an assistant project scientist at BIDS, UC Berkeley, USA, working on open source algorithms for processing computed tomography (CT) 3D images.

I received my master’s and doctorate degrees from UNESP, Brazil, applying image processing tools to tackle challenges in materials science and geochronology.

I am a core developer of scikit-image, and an open source and free software enthusiast since my first contact with Linux, in 2000, contributing to several projects and events in Latin America and Europe.

I also worked as a postdoctoral fellow at the State University of Campinas, Brazil, and the TU Bergakademie Freiberg, Germany, where I created pytracks and wrote Octave - Your first steps on scientific programming (in Brazilian Portuguese).
